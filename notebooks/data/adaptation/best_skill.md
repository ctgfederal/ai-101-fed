# scrap-coder

## Knowledge
- Codes: DIM_OOT, SURF_DEF, MAT_FLAW, MACH_SET, TOOL_WEAR, OP_ERR, CONTAM.
- Read the line, pick the single best-fitting code.

## Antipatterns
- Do not invent codes outside the seven.
